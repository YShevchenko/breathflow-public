Frequently Asked Questions

Is BreathFlow really free?

Yes. Three breathing techniques (Box Breathing, 4-7-8, and Physiological Sigh) are completely free with no time limits or restrictions. You can optionally unlock 7 additional premium techniques with a one-time purchase of $6.99 — no subscription required.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Open BreathFlow and go to the premium section. Tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed into the same account at no additional cost.

Does BreathFlow collect my data?

No. BreathFlow collects zero personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no ads. All your settings and session history stay on your device. The only external SDK is TikTok Events for install attribution, which fires once and only if you allow tracking via Apple's ATT prompt. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What breathing techniques are included?

The free tier includes Box Breathing, 4-7-8 Breathing, and Physiological Sigh. The premium unlock adds 7 more techniques including Wim Hof, Alternate Nostril Breathing, Buteyko, and others.

Can I use BreathFlow offline?

Absolutely. BreathFlow works entirely offline. No internet connection is needed to use any feature, including premium techniques after purchase.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and follow the steps for the BreathFlow purchase.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

We would love to hear from you. Please email us at aifluencer@pm.me with a description of the issue or your idea. Include your iOS version and device model if reporting a bug.
