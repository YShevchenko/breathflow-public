BreathFlow - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

BreathFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

Health Data

BreathFlow stores the following health-related data locally on your device only:

  • Breathing session history — technique used, session duration, and date
  • Streak data — consecutive days of practice

This data is used solely to track your breathing practice and display your personal progress within the app. It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

BreathFlow does not access Apple Health, Google Fit, or any external health platform.

What We Store Locally

  • Breathing session history (technique, duration, date)
  • App preferences (haptics, sound, theme, duration)
  • Streak data
  • Purchase status

This data is stored only on your device using local storage. It is never sent to any server.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed.

To delete all your data:

  • Uninstall the app — this permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • Alternatively, go to your device Settings → Apps → BreathFlow → Clear Data to erase all app data without uninstalling.

No data exists on any server, so there is nothing to request deletion of externally.

No Accounts

BreathFlow does not require or support user accounts. No email, name, or personal information is ever requested.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information.

No Network Access

BreathFlow works entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests except for in-app purchase verification handled by the platform.

Children's Privacy

BreathFlow does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Health Disclaimer

BreathFlow is a wellness tool and is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
